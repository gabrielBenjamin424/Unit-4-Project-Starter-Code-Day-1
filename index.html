Michael Collins (astronaut)

Who is Michael Collins?

  • Michael Collins is an astronaut, retired major general of the air force, and former president of the National Air and Space Museum.
  • He was a member of the Apollo 11 mission with Neil Armstrong and Buzz Aldrin.

Apollo 11 Mission

During the Apollo 11 mission, Michael Collins did not actually go on the moon, instead he piloted the Columbia. He was mostly worried that he would have to leave the others behind.
